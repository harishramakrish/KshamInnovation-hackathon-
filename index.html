Our future plans

Augmented Reality (AR) Enhancements

Smart glasses are likely to incorporate more advanced AR capabilities, allowing users to overlay digital information seamlessly onto their real-world environment. This could include enhanced navigation with AR overlays, interactive virtual objects, real-time language translation, and immersive gaming experiences.

Health and Fitness Monitoring

Future smart glasses may integrate more robust health and fitness monitoring features. This could include biometric sensors for tracking heart rate, blood oxygen levels, and other health metrics. Smart glasses could also provide real-time feedback during workouts, offer personalized health insights, and integrate with health apps and services.

Enhanced Connectivitiy

Smart glasses may offer enhanced connectivity options, such as 5G support for faster and more reliable internet connectivity. This would enable seamless streaming of high-definition content, video calls, and access to cloud-based services without latency issues.

Advanced Display Technology

Future smart glasses may feature more advanced display technologies, such as micro-LED or OLED displays, offering higher resolutions, wider field of view, and improved brightness and clarity. This would enhance the visual experience for users, especially for AR applications and multimedia content.
